# NodePilot — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 1, 2026_

NodePilot ("the app") is a local-network tool that lets you control your own Windows PC from
your own phone. In short: **NodePilot sends nothing to the developer, to the cloud, or to any third party, and
sells nothing.** Any data the app handles moves directly between your own paired devices on your
local network — never to us.

## 1. Information we collect
NodePilot does not collect any personal information and does not send any data to the developer,
to the cloud, or to any third party. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising,
and no telemetry sent to us.

## 2. How NodePilot works (your data stays on your devices)
NodePilot connects your phone and your PC directly over your local Wi‑Fi / LAN using an encrypted
(TLS) connection. All information — system status (CPU, memory, etc.), audio and media controls,
remote input, screen images, files you choose to transfer, clipboard contents, and any other
content you access — is exchanged only between your own paired devices. None of it passes through
the developer's servers, because NodePilot has no servers.

## 3. Information stored locally
To function, NodePilot stores a small amount of data locally on your own devices, such as: the
pairing token that authorizes your phone, the certificate fingerprint used to verify your PC, and
your settings and per‑device permissions. This data never leaves your devices and is used only to
maintain the trusted connection between them.

## 4. Permissions the app uses, and why
The app requests only the capabilities needed for its features, used solely to serve your own
paired phone over your LAN:
- **Local network / act as a server** — to accept the connection from your phone.
- **Full‑trust / system access** — to read system status and control the PC (audio, media, remote
  input, screen, power) as you direct from your phone.
- **File libraries (Documents, Pictures, Videos, Music)** — only to let you browse and transfer
  files between your PC and your own phone, at your request.

Content accessed through these permissions is transmitted only to your own paired phone and never
to the developer or any third party.

## 5. Data sharing
We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any of your data to anyone. There are no third‑party
services, SDKs, trackers, or advertising networks in NodePilot.

## 6. Security
Connections between your devices are encrypted with TLS and authenticated with a per‑device token
established during pairing (a 6‑digit code plus an on‑PC confirmation). Sensitive capabilities are
disabled by default and must be enabled explicitly for each device.

## 7. Children's privacy
NodePilot is a general‑purpose utility and is not directed at children. It does not knowingly
collect any information from anyone, including children.

## 8. Changes to this policy
If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and publish the new version
with the app.
